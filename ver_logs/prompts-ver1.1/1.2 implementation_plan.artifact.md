## Verification Plan

### Automated Tests
- Run existing/new tests for `Manga.isAdultContent()`.
- Command: `./gradlew domain:testDebugUnitTest --tests "tachiyomi.domain.manga.model.MangaContentFilterTest"`

### Manual Verification
1. **Extension Filter Mode**:
   - Change mode to "Block all" and verify no extensions/sources are visible.
   - Change mode to "Block NSFW" and verify NSFW extensions are hidden.
   - Change mode to "Allow selected" and verify only whitelisted extensions are visible.
2. **Strict Content Filter**:
   - Toggle "Strict content filter" and verify that adult manga are filtered from browse results.
3. **Manga Detail Fail-Safe**:
   - Attempt to open a blocked manga via deep link or history. Verify the blocked placeholder is shown.
4. **Reader Fail-Safe**:
   - Attempt to open a chapter of a blocked manga. Verify it fails gracefully with an error message.
